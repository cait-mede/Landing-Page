I've always had a passion for helping others. I obtained my B.A. in psychology at Temple University and my M.S. in mental health counseling at the Philadelphia College of Osteopathic Medicine. After graduation, I began working as a therapist, where I walked alongside people while they navigated challenging times in their lives. Through this experience, I fine-tuned my communication, empathy, and analytical skills. However, after a few years I found that I was no longer fully engaged and excited about my future career path.

I was seeking a new way to continue improving the lives of others while also channeling my desire for novelty, logical reasoning, and teamwork. Through my self-exploration and research, I found out about software development as a career. I immediately felt drawn in by the idea of using my problem-solving skills to build a program. I wanted to see what it was all about, so I began teaching myself how to code with the use of online resources. After a few months of exploring the possibilities of programming, I knew I had found the career I wanted to pursue.

I was so excited about getting started that I decided to take a risk and apply for a coding bootcamp. Due to their impressive reputation and mission to diversify the tech industry, I chose Tech Elevator. Once I was accepted, I applied for the scholarship they offer, called Represent Tech. They chose me as the Philadelphia, Spring 2021 Recipient. I couldn't be more grateful to receive this opportunity as well as all of the knowledge I have accumulated during the program. I can't wait to contribute to tech community. Thank you so much for reading!
