Rock Climbing: Find me at a local rock climbing gym or outdoor climbing area! I love the problem-solving aspect of rock climbing as well as getting to experience myself level up and get stronger!

Flow Arts: Not many people know what flow arts are. In general, it encompasses the emerging movement-based artforms that integrate dance and creative exploration of movement with skill-based prop manipulation. This artform helps me to de-stress and connect with other creatives! Usually I dance with one or more hula-hoops.

Fire Spinning: Sometimes I light my props on fire (after taking many safety precautions) and dance with them. This is a very empowering, exhilarating, and humbling activity. Over the summer, I plan to take classes to learn how to eat and breathe fire. Wish me luck!

Traveling: I love traveling and exploring new natural environments such as National Parks. My favorite landscapes are forests and deserts. My goal this year is to travel abroad for the first time.

Cooking: Making an amazing plant-based meal from scratch warms my soul. Currently, I would say my favorite dish to make is coconut-red curry and naan. Yum!
